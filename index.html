Astral Odyssey

An epic space platformer.

Synopsis: In a life-long quest of unsuccessful search for the Grand Unifying Theory, an astrophysicist decides that to make more progress, he must explore beyond the home planet Earth. After stealing a spaceship from NASA, he embarks on a courageous, fun, and educational journey, full of both achievements and surprises...

Note: Ideation for this game commenced on 12 September 2023

Development for this game commenced 1 month and two days later, on 14 October 2023

Release was the 4th of December, 2 months and 22 days (83 days) after the initial ideation, and 1 month and 20 days (51 days) after development started.
